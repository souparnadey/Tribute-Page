Jensen Huang: An Inspirational Journey

Jen-Hsun "Jensen" Huang is an American businessman, electrical engineer, and philanthropist who is the founder, president and chief executive officer of Nvidia. The son of Taiwanese immigrants, Huang moved from Taiwan to the United States in his childhood.

After graduating from university, Huang served as the director of CoreWare at LSI Logic and a microprocessor designer at Advanced Micro Devices (AMD). In 1993, at 30 years old, he went on to start his own business, where he co-founded Nvidia with Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem and became its CEO and president. As of 2024, Huang has been Nvidia's chief executive for over three decades.
